<h3>An N-number can be in any of these formats</h3>
<ul>
<li>One to five digits (N12345)</li>

<li>One to three digits followed by two letters (N123AZ)</li>

<h3>N-numbers do not have</h3>
<ul>
<li>A zero (0) as the first number</li>
